Short Biography [CV]

    Dr. Hao Zhong is the founding member and Big Data Scientist in EZFintel (Shenzhen) Ltd., a FinTech company that leverages Big Data and Artificial Intelligence to provide Risk Management software, platforms and customized solutions for the Financial Services industry. Dr. Zhong received his PhD in Management (concentration on data mining) from the Rutgers Business School at Rutgers University. Prior to joining Rutgers, he received his M.S. in Computer Science from York University in Toronto and B.S. in Electonic Information Science and Technology from University of Science and Technology of China (USTC). His general areas of research are data mining, business analytics, and recommender systems, with a focus on developing effective and efficient data mining techniques for emerging and challenging business applications. He has published prolifically in top venues of data mining and operations research, including journals (ANOR, IEEE TMC, IEEE TKDE) and conference proceedings (ACM SIGKDD, IEEE ICDM, SIAM SDM, etc).
