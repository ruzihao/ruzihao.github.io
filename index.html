    Dr. Hao (Howard) Zhong is currently a tenure-track assistant professor in the Information & Operations Management (IOM) department at ESCP Business School since Octobor 2020. He alse serves as an adjunct lecturer at Zhejiang University International Business School (ZIBS). He was also a founding member and Big Data Scientist at EZFintel (Shenzhen) Ltd., a start-up FinTech company that leverages Big Data and AI to provide risk management softwares, platforms and solutions for top financial institutions in China. He received his Ph.D. in Management (conc. data mining) from Rutgers, the State University of New Jersey, under the supervision of Prof. Hui Xiong. He has internship experiences from American Express (via DIA Associates), Philips Research - NA and Bell Labs, Alcatel-Lucent during the period of his doctoral study. His general areas of research are data mining, venture analytics, and FinTech. He has published prolifically in top venues of data mining and operations research, including refereed journals (ANOR, IEEE TMC, IEEE TKDE) and conference proceedings (ACM SIGKDD, IEEE ICDM, SIAM SDM, etc). He served as co-president of USTC Alumni Association of Greater New York in 2016-2017.
